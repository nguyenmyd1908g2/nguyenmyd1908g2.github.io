The 1988 animated film My Neighbor Totoro shows this idea very well as two young sisters move into a countryside home with their father after their mother is hospitalized. The woods surrounding the home have some very interesting beings living in them and these particular entities can only be seen by children. The film starts with Mei and Satsuki, along with their father who is driving, on the road in the countryside to a new home

This immediately ties into Aryanism as they are making their to what is clearly a farming community and they have come from a big city, which ends up making only one, very brief, appearance in the film. The young girls are filled with a genuine children’s enthusiasm as they wave and greet the people they pass. When they reach the house, the girls’ gusto doesn’t pause for a second as they explore each and every corner.
